TEAM:

At Gardenshelters creating garden space is not only work but also a passion. We build arbors, which give gardens a specific, individual character. They are a place to relax, work, meet, rest. We design, advise, implement, assemble, providing fully comprehensive service working closely with graphic designers, carpenters and fitters.

DESIGN:

On the website you can see our most popular models in the most frequently ordered dimensions. There is also a possibility to adjust the shelters to your individual needs and aesthetics. We advise, design and price without additional charges.

MATERIALS:

All our garden shelters are made of C-24 certified Scandinavian wood fully treated and dried to help improve stability and straightness, often specified by architects and used in building projects where premium strength is required. This treatment prevents the timber from rot, decay and insect infestation for up to 10 years. We attach great importance to the connecting elements of our structures using only suitable types of screws and fittings to maintain maximum rigidity and robustness. All our garden shelters are individually hand painted as standard, we have a comprehensive range of high quality timber treatment products from Drewnochron and Trikkula.

DELIVERY:

Standard UK delivery by our supplier within 8 weeks. Delivery costs to mainland England, Wales and Scotland are already included in the price.

ASSEMBLY SERVICE:

Where delivery needs to be arranged, you will be contacted to arrange a date and time that's convenient for you. Our garden shelters are made as DIY products. However, upon your request our experienced assembly team will be happy to install it for you. Installation charges are indicated under each product.
